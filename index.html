About OPCF-Africa

To combat violence against elderly persons in Africa through the establishment of rescue centers, research, education, advocacy, entrepreneurial initiatives, and compassionate caregiving.

Organization

Older Persons Care Foundation-Africa (OPCF-Africa) is a Community Based Organization dedicated to protecting elderly persons at risk of violence.

Our Mission

To combat violence against elderly persons through rescue centers, research, education, advocacy, entrepreneurship, and compassionate caregiving.

Our Purpose

Advancing Jesus Christ's redemptive mission by providing refuge and care to elderly persons facing violence.

Our Vision

Beginning in Kenya and expanding across Africa to protect vulnerable elderly persons and restore dignity and safety.

Our Objectives

OPCF-Africa advances Christ’s redemptive mission by protecting elderly persons at risk of violence and restoring dignity, safety and care.

Rescue Centers

Establish safe rescue centers that serve as family-like homes for elderly persons facing threats of violence.

Discipleship Training

Develop Christ-centered training programs for caregivers and residents focusing on discipleship and service.

Research

Conduct multidisciplinary research to develop culturally relevant solutions addressing violence against elderly persons.

Advocacy

Advocate for the dignity and rights of elderly persons through publications, digital media, and public engagement.
